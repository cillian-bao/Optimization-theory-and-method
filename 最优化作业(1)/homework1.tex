作业(1):\\
(1)(2)单纯形法
(1):
\[
\begin{split}
min \quad -9x_1-16x_2\\
\begin{aligned}
s.t.\quad &x_1+4x_2+x_3=80,\\
     &2x_1+3x_2+x_4=90,\\
     &x_j \geq 0,j=1,\cdots,4.\\
\end{aligned}
\end{split}
\]
(2):
\[
\begin{split}
max \quad x_1+3x_2\\
\begin{aligned}
s.t.\quad &2x_1+3x_2+x_3=6,\\
     &-x_1+x_2+x_4=1,\\
     &x_j \geq 0,j=1,\cdots,4.\\
\end{aligned}
\end{split}
\]
